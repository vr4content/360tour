title

Here you can see how it looks your 3D model integrated on the webpage, however, this is a web AR experience made for mobiles phones. To bring this 3D model to your environment visit this page with your mobile web browser.

how to use?

1. Scan this QR code or write manually the actual url on your mobile browser.

2. Click on the icon over the 3d model to bring the object to your space.

3. Move your mobile around to scan your surroundings and place your 3d model.

4. Record a video or take pictures from your experiences to share with others.

embed code

Copy and paste this embed code on to your web editor to bring this 3d ar experience to any web page
